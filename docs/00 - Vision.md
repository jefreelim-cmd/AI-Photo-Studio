\# AI Photo Studio - Vision



\## Mission



Build a professional AI Photo Studio using ComfyUI focused on restoring, enhancing and preserving photographs while maintaining historical authenticity.



The objective is not to generate artwork.



The objective is to restore photographs so they appear as though they were captured using modern photographic technology while preserving the original people, memories and historical context.



\---



\## Goals



\- Restore old family photographs.

\- Preserve identity and authenticity.

\- Improve image quality using modern AI techniques.

\- Build reusable, modular ComfyUI workflows.

\- Benchmark every workflow before adopting it.

\- Build a restoration toolkit that can evolve as AI technology improves.



\---



\## Long-Term Vision



Create a documented, benchmarked and production-quality AI restoration workstation that produces consistent, repeatable and high-quality results.
